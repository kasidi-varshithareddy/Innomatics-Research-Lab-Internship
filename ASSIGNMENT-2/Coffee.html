Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a coffee drink that is made with espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It is typically served in a small cup and is topped with a dusting of cocoa powder.

Latte

Latte is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. It is typically served in a tall glass and is topped with a small amount of milk foam.

Price: $4.00

Macchiato

Macchiato is a coffee drink made with espresso and a small amount of steamed milk. It is typically served in a small cup and is topped with a dollop of milk foam.
